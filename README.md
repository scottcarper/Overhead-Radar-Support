## FAQ

### No aircraft appear on the map

Overhead Radar displays aircraft detected via ADS-B broadcasts near your selected radar center.

If no aircraft appear:

• Make sure you have a network connection  
• Try increasing the radar range  
• Aircraft coverage depends on available ADS-B data in your area  

---

### Aircraft movement looks delayed

Aircraft positions update periodically based on live ADS-B broadcasts.  
Overhead Radar smoothly interpolates positions between updates to simulate motion.

---

### Radar is centered in the wrong location

If location access is enabled, Overhead Radar may center the radar on your current location.

Check:

iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Overhead Radar → While Using

You can also recenter the radar using the **scope button**.

---

### Flight details not loading

Flight detail enrichment may require additional network requests.  
If details fail to load, check your connection and try again.

---

## Privacy

Overhead Radar uses location only to display aircraft flying near your current location while the app is in use.

Overhead Radar does not require an account and does not collect or sell personal data.

Aircraft position data is sourced from publicly available ADS-B broadcasts via third-party aviation data providers.
